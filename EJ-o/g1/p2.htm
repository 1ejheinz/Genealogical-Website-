Narrative Biography

Charles migrated to America in 1878, some 30 years after his aunt and four uncles. He was a farmer, and was a Catholic. He married Margaret German in 1858 and they lived in Wady Petre, Stark County, Illinois. Charles H. Heinz disappeared in 1891, before the birth of his youngest daughter. His wife reared their family alone, by working as a practical nurse and also did paperhanging.
